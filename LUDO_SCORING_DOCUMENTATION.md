## 📝 IMPORTANT NOTES

### Dice Roll Notes:
- **First six of game**: Gets extra +5 bonus (only once per game)
- **Consecutive sixes**: Bonus = 2 × consecutive count (if > 1)
  - 2nd consecutive: +4 (2 × 2)
  - 3rd consecutive: +6 (2 × 3)
  - 4th consecutive: +8 (2 × 4)
- **Roll 1**: Gets +1 lucky bonus
- **Normal moves**: No points, only bonuses count

### Move Notes:
- **Normal move**: No points awarded
- **Kill**: +10 points when you capture opponent piece
- **Home reach**: +15 points when piece reaches home
- **Perfect move**: +5 bonus when reaching home from position ≤6
- **Safe square**: +2 points for landing on safe position
- **Multiple bonuses**: All applicable bonuses stack
